Indian Market Pricing Model

Our pricing engine systematically converts international spot prices to Indian domestic retail rates by accounting for currency exchange, import duties, and local taxation laws.

Sequence Component Adjustment Factor
1 Global Benchmark COMEX Silver Futures (SI=F)
2 Currency Exchange Real-time USD to INR Conversion
3 Import Duty +6% (Basic Customs + AIDC)
4 Market Premium +10% (Logistics, Dealer Margins, MCX Spread)
5 Local Taxation +3% GST (Goods & Service Tax)
